About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Pharmacological Sciences in Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in New York, NY. I am experienced in working with high-dimensional biomedical data using various approaches including exploratory analysis, multivariate statistics, network analysis, clustering and classification. I also make interactive data visualizations and web applications to let the data speak for themselves and allow users to query the data. Check out my projects and publications for more details.
